About Me

My name is Stu! Thanks for dropping by my page!

So a little bit about me...I've always got an idea or two brewing and most people who know me would say I've got a good head on my shoulders. I'm pretty passionate about helping people; whether that means volunteering at a local food bank and or crafting a cool new brand for a growing company.

I also like to learn new stuff so I've been acquiring new skills basically from birth, and not to toot my own horn but I've gotten pretty good at stuff like web development, online marketing, and business research. Does that make me nerdy?

Oh, and I love to spend time with friends. I guess you could say I'm a foodie since most of the time when I get together with friends, there's food involved. Sometimes it's a chili cookoff (no beans – we're in Texas after all), sometimes it's a margarita and salsa competition and sometimes it's just a good old fashioned pot luck dinner.

No matter what I'm doing, I always put my whole heart into it. That's sort of baked into my DNA. Anyway, thanks for dropping by my page!
